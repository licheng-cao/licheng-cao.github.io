I am an associate professor and master's supervisor at the School of Marine Science and Technology, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan). My teaching and research focus on marine geology, sedimentary geochemistry, provenance analysis, paleogeographic reconstruction, and source-to-sink processes in the South China Sea and Southeast Asia.

This website collects my research projects, fieldwork records, publications, team information, CV, and occasional writing on education and research.
