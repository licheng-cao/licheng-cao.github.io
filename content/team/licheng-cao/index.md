Associate Professor, School of Marine Science and Technology, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan).

## Research Interests

- Paleogeographic reconstruction and paleo-river evolution.
- Provenance analysis and single-grain analytical methods.
- Geological evolution of the South China Sea and Southeast Asia.
- Source-to-sink systems and sedimentary geochemistry.
