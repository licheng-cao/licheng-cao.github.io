## Research Areas

My teaching and research focus on marine geology and sedimentary geochemistry.

### Research Interests

- Paleogeographic reconstruction and paleo-river evolution.
- Provenance analysis and single-grain analysis.
- Geological evolution of the China Seas and Southeast Asia.

### Previous Research Topics

- Cenozoic topographic evolution and large-river development along the southeastern Tibetan Plateau, including the Pearl River and Red River.
- Basin-to-regional source-to-sink system evolution in the South China Sea from rifting to post-rift stages.
- Transition from a Mesozoic active margin to a Cenozoic passive margin around the South China Sea region.

### Current Research Directions

- Paleogeographic reconstruction of the South China Sea and Southeast Asia.
- Paleo-Pacific subduction along the South China-Sundaland margin.
- New provenance analysis methods.
- Transfer and preservation of provenance signals.
