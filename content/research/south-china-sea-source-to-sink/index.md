## Overview

This project examines how sediment was generated, transported, and preserved from continental source areas to marginal and deep-water basins of the South China Sea. The work integrates heavy minerals, geochemistry, detrital zircon U-Pb ages, and regional basin analysis.

## Key Questions

- How did sediment routing systems evolve from syn-rift to post-rift stages?
- Which continental source areas supplied sand to the northern and central South China Sea?
- How are provenance signals modified during long-distance transport?
