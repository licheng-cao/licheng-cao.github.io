Research pages are organized as project records. Each project can include a short overview, research questions, explanatory figures, related publications, collaborators, funding information, and tags.

## Current Research Directions

- Paleogeographic reconstruction of the South China Sea and Southeast Asia.
- Paleo-Pacific subduction along the South China-Sundaland margin.
- New approaches in provenance analysis and single-grain analysis.
- Transfer and preservation of provenance signals in sediment routing systems.
