## Overview

This project focuses on paleogeographic reconstruction around the South China Sea, including the Palawan continental terrane, Borneo, and the South China-Sundaland margin. Provenance records from sedimentary units are used to test regional tectonic and paleogeographic models.

## Key Questions

- What provenance connections existed between the Palawan continental terrane and the northern South China Sea margin?
- How did the Mesozoic active margin evolve into the Cenozoic passive margin system?
- What sedimentary evidence records Paleo-Pacific subduction and its termination?
